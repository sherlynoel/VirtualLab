Knapsack | Dynamic Programming



Problem Statement : A thief is robbing a store and can carry a maximal weight of W into his knapsack. There are n items available in the store and weight of ith item is wi and its profit is pi. What items should the thief take?



Knapsack | Dynamic Programming

What does it mean?

In knapsack problem, we have a knapsack i.e. a bag and few objects having some weight with a particular value/profit. The capacity of the bag is given and the objects are to be placed in the bag such that maximum profit can be made. The term 0/1 refers to either we take the object as a whole i.e 1 or don't take it at all i.e 0. We use the dynamic programming approach to calculate the maximum profit obtainable

Concept

In knapsack problem, we have a bag with a certain capacity and have n items from i1 to in having weight as w1 to wn with particular value/profit as p1 to pn. We have to put items into the knapsack such that the capacity is not exceeded and profit made is maximum.
